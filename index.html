About

WEEEKLY (위클리) (formerly known as PlayM Girls and FAVE Girls) is a seven-member girl group under Play M Entertainment consisting of Lee Soojin, Shin Jiyoon, Monday, Park Soeun, Lee Jaehee, Jihan, and Zoa. They debuted in June 30, 2020 with their debut album ‘We Are’ which features the title track Tag Me (@Me).

WEEEKLY Fandom Name: Daileee (데일리)
“Without ‘Daileee (Daily)’ ‘Weeekly’ cannot exist.”

It became official on August 23, 2020.

Albums

We Are (2020)


The first mini album by Weeekly. It was released on June 30, 2020 with “Tag Me (@Me)” serving as the title track.

We Can (2020)


The second mini album by Weeekly. It was released on October 13, 2020 with the song “Zig Zag” serving as the album’s title track.

The physical album comes in two versions: “Orb” and “Wave”.

We Play (2021)


The third album by Weeekly. It was released on March 17, 2021 with “After School” serving as the album’s title track.

The Physical album comes in two versions: “Jump” and “Up”.

Play Game: Holiday (2021)


The fourth mini album by Weeekly. It was released on August 4, 2021 with “Holiday Party” serving as the album’s title track.

The physical album comes in two versions: E World and M World.
